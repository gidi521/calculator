How to Use the Professional Age Calculator

  1. Exact Age Calculation: Enter DOB + optional target date + calendar type → get precise age breakdown
  2. Birthday Countdown: Enter your birthday (MM/DD) → get days until next birthday + future age
  3. Age Difference: Enter two dates → get exact gap (which date is older/younger)

Accuracy Notes

  • Accounts for leap years (29 February) – fixes common age calculation errors
  • Uses actual month lengths (28/29/30/31 days) instead of average 30 days
  • Julian calendar conversion adjusts for 10-13 day historical differences
  • Time calculations use UTC to avoid timezone discrepancies
