Fraction Calculator




The Fraction Calculator allows you to perform basic arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) with fractions. Simply enter the numerators and denominators of two fractions, select the operation you want to perform, and click the "Calculate" button to see the result.

For example, if you want to add two fractions, enter the numerators and denominators of both fractions in the respective input fields, select the "Add" operation, and click "Calculate" to see the sum of the fractions.

Keep in mind that fractions should be entered in the form of numerator/denominator, and both numerators and denominators should be integers.
