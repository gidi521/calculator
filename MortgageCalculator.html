Core Primary Mortgage Calculators

1. Standard Monthly Mortgage Payment

Explain: The core standard mortgage calculator computes fixed monthly principal + interest using the official amortization formula. Ideal for checking basic house loan monthly costs for 15-year / 20-year / 30-year fixed loans.

2. Mortgage Refinance Savings

Explain: Compare your existing mortgage with a new lower interest rate refinance. Shows monthly payment reduction and long-term total interest savings to decide whether refinancing is profitable.

3. Down Payment Impact Calculator

Explain: Test how 5%, 10%, 15%, or 20% down payment changes your loan size and monthly payment. Critical for avoiding PMI by reaching the 20% down threshold.

4. Home Affordability Estimator

Explain: Uses standard front-end / back-end DTI debt-to-income ratios to calculate the maximum home price you can safely afford based on your salary and current debts.

5. PMI Private Mortgage Insurance

Explain: PMI applies when down payment < 20%. This tool calculates your extra monthly insurance cost and helps you decide to increase down payment to remove PMI.

6. Extra Payment Loan Payoff

Explain: Shows how adding small extra payments every month can drastically reduce total interest paid and shorten your full loan payoff time.

7. Rent vs Buy Comparison

Explain: Professional comparison to evaluate whether continuing to rent or purchasing property creates better long-term wealth and monthly budget balance.

8. Cash Out Refinance Equity

Explain: Calculate available home equity for cash-out refinancing, home renovation, debt consolidation, or investment funding based on property value and LTV limits.

9. Property Tax Monthly Estimator

Explain: Adds real estate property tax into your total housing expense for accurate full monthly budget planning beyond just loan payment.

10. Homeowners Insurance Monthly

Explain: Calculate average home insurance monthly cost to complete your full PITI (Principal + Interest + Tax + Insurance) mortgage budget.

11. 15 Year vs 30 Year Mortgage

Explain: Professional side-by-side comparison showing monthly payment difference and massive total interest savings when choosing a shorter 15-year fixed mortgage.

12. HELOC Equity Line Estimator

Explain: Estimate your usable home equity line of credit (HELOC) amount for renovations, emergency funds, or investment leverage.
