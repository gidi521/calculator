The Body Fat Calculator estimates the percentage of body fat based on weight, height, age, and gender. It is a useful tool for assessing health and fitness levels. The calculation is based on various formulas that take into account these factors to provide an approximation of body fat percentage.

It is important to note that body fat percentage can vary based on factors such as muscle mass, bone density, and overall body composition. This calculator provides a rough estimate and should not be used as a definitive measure of body fat percentage.
