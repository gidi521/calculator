How To Use

  1. Select gender and enter age
  2. Enter height, weight, waist, neck, and hip (female)

  4. Use results to track fitness & weight goals

Body Fat Categories (General)

  • Essential Fat: 2–5% (men) | 10–13% (women)
  • Athletes: 6–13% | 14–20%
  • Fitness: 14–17% | 21–24%
  • Acceptable: 18–24% | 25–31%
  • Obese: >25% | >32%
