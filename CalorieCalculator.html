Calorie Calculator






Your daily calorie needs are: calories

The Calorie Calculator helps estimate the number of calories you need to consume each day to maintain your current weight based on your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. Maintaining an appropriate calorie intake is essential for managing your weight and overall health. Here are the general activity levels used in calculations:

Sedentary: Little or no exercise
Lightly active: Light exercise/sports 1-3 days/week
Moderately active: Moderate exercise/sports 3-5 days/week
Very active: Hard exercise/sports 6-7 days a week
Super active: Very hard exercise/sports & physical job

Please note that this is a rough estimate, and individual needs may vary based on additional factors such as body composition, metabolism, and specific health goals. It's always best to consult with a healthcare provider or nutritionist for personalized advice.
