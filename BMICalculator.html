BMI Calculator



The BMI Calculator helps you assess your body fat based on your height and weight. Simply enter your height in centimeters and weight in kilograms, then click "Calculate" to see your Body Mass Index (BMI) and weight status.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 ≤ BMI < 24.9
  • Overweight: 25.0 ≤ BMI < 29.9
  • Obese: BMI ≥ 30.0
