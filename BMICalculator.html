BMI Calculator



Your BMI is:

BMI, short for Body Mass Index, is a commonly used method for measuring the degree of obesity in a person. It calculates by dividing a person's weight (in kilograms) by the square of their height (in meters). BMI is a rough method used to assess whether a person falls within a healthy weight range. According to the World Health Organization (WHO) standards, BMI is classified as follows:

BMI less than 18.5: Underweight
BMI between 18.5 and 24.9: Normal weight
BMI between 25 and 29.9: Overweight
BMI greater than or equal to 30: Obesity

However, it is important to note that BMI is only a rough measure and does not account for other factors of body composition such as the ratio of muscle to fat, age, gender, and race. Therefore, it should be used cautiously as a general indicator of health.
