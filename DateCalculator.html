Date Calculator




The Date Calculator allows you to perform various date calculations, including adding or subtracting days, months, or years from a given date. It provides a convenient tool for managing dates and calculating intervals between them.

For example, if you want to add 5 days to a specific date, select the date, '+', enter '5' in the amount field, select 'Days', and click 'Calculate' to see the result.
