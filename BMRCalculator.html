BMR Calculator





Your BMR is: calories/day

BMR, or Basal Metabolic Rate, is an estimate of the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic physiological functions at rest, such as breathing, circulation, and cell production. Knowing your BMR can help you manage your weight by understanding how many calories your body requires to function properly.

To calculate your BMR, use the Harris-Benedict equation:
For men: BMR = 88.362 + (13.397 * weight in kg) + (4.799 * height in cm) - (5.677 * age in years)
For women: BMR = 447.593 + (9.247 * weight in kg) + (3.098 * height in cm) - (4.330 * age in years)

This calculator can help you estimate your daily calorie needs by taking into account your weight, height, age, and gender. Keep in mind that this is a rough estimate and actual calorie needs may vary based on factors such as muscle mass, activity level, and overall health.
