BMR Calculator





The BMR Calculator helps you estimate the number of calories your body needs to perform basic functions while at rest. Simply enter your age, gender, weight, and height, and click the "Calculate" button to see your BMR.

For example, if you want to know your daily calorie needs to maintain your current weight, enter your age, select your gender, input your weight in kilograms, and height in centimeters. Click "Calculate" to see your BMR.

Keep in mind that the BMR is a baseline number. Your actual daily calorie needs will be higher depending on your activity level.
