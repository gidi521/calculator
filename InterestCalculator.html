Interest Calculator




The Interest Calculator calculates the total amount of interest earned or paid over a specified period based on the principal amount, interest rate, and duration of the investment or loan. Simply enter the required information and click "Calculate" to see the result.

For example, if you're considering investing a certain amount at a specific interest rate for a certain duration, you can use this calculator to estimate the total interest earned over that period.

Similarly, if you're planning to take out a loan, you can use this calculator to estimate the total interest paid over the loan term.
