Kashmiri Diet Calculator


Kashmiri Food Culture Meets Nutritional Precision


The Kashmiri Diet Calculator is a nutritional tool designed to estimate daily caloric needs using traditional Kashmiri dietary patterns as a guide. It considers user inputs such as age, gender, height, weight, and activity level to provide personalized dietary advice. Whether you're aiming for weight loss, muscle gain, or simply maintaining a healthy lifestyle, understanding your daily nutritional intake is crucial.


What is the Kashmiri Diet?


The Kashmiri diet is rich in flavor and tradition, featuring staples such as rice, lamb, chicken, lotus stem, curd, and spices like saffron, cardamom, and fennel. Dishes such as Rogan Josh, Yakhni, and Dum Aloo are high in both taste and nutritional value. This calculator helps translate traditional food preferences into modern nutritional goals.


How the Calculator Works


Using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, the calculator estimates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). It then multiplies this number by your activity factor to provide your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). This TDEE represents the number of calories you need to maintain your current weight.

  • If your goal is to lose weight, consume 10–20% fewer calories than your TDEE.
  • If your goal is to gain weight, consume 10–20% more calories than your TDEE.

Traditional Kashmiri Foods and Their Calorie Values


Below are sample caloric values for common Kashmiri dishes:

  • Rogan Josh (200g): ~350 kcal
  • Haak Saag (100g): ~80 kcal
  • Dum Aloo (150g): ~220 kcal
  • Plain Rice (1 cup): ~210 kcal
  • Sheermal Bread (1 piece): ~180 kcal

Combining these foods in the right proportion can help maintain nutritional balance while preserving cultural taste.
