Mathematical Formulas Reference

1. Basic Arithmetic Operations

Addition: a + b Subtraction: a - b Multiplication: a × b Division: a ÷ b Modulus: a % b (remainder) Exponentiation: a^b or Math.pow(a,b) Square Root: √a or Math.sqrt(a) Cube Root: ∛a or Math.cbrt(a) Reciprocal: 1/a Percentage: (a/100) × b

2. Trigonometric Functions (Radians)

Sine: sin(θ) = opposite/hypotenuse Cosine: cos(θ) = adjacent/hypotenuse Tangent: tan(θ) = opposite/adjacent Inverse Sine: sin⁻¹(x) = arcsin(x) Inverse Cosine: cos⁻¹(x) = arccos(x) Inverse Tangent: tan⁻¹(x) = arctan(x) Hyperbolic Sine: sinh(x) = (eˣ - e⁻ˣ)/2 Hyperbolic Cosine: cosh(x) = (eˣ + e⁻ˣ)/2 Hyperbolic Tangent: tanh(x) = sinh(x)/cosh(x) Degree to Radian: radians = degrees × π/180 Radian to Degree: degrees = radians × 180/π

3. Logarithmic & Exponential Functions

Natural Logarithm: ln(x) = logₑ(x) Common Logarithm: log₁₀(x) Binary Logarithm: log₂(x) Exponential: eˣ = exp(x) Logarithm Change of Base: log_b(x) = ln(x)/ln(b) Properties: ln(ab) = ln(a) + ln(b) ln(a/b) = ln(a) - ln(b) ln(aᵇ) = b × ln(a) e^(ln(x)) = x

4. Engineering Formulas

Ohm's Law: V = I × R (Voltage = Current × Resistance) I = V/R (Current = Voltage/Resistance) R = V/I (Resistance = Voltage/Current) P = V × I (Power = Voltage × Current) P = I² × R (Power = Current² × Resistance) P = V²/R (Power = Voltage²/Resistance) Base Conversion: Decimal to Binary: divide by 2, record remainders Decimal to Octal: divide by 8, record remainders Decimal to Hex: divide by 16, record remainders (10-15 = A-F)

5. Physics Formulas

Kinematics (Constant Acceleration): v = u + at (final velocity) s = ut + ½at² (displacement) v² = u² + 2as (velocity squared) Force & Newton's Laws: F = ma (Force = mass × acceleration) W = mg (Weight = mass × gravity) F = G×m₁×m₂/r² (Gravitational force) Work & Energy: W = F × d × cos(θ) (Work) KE = ½mv² (Kinetic Energy) PE = mgh (Potential Energy) E = mc² (Einstein's mass-energy equivalence) Momentum: p = mv (Momentum = mass × velocity) J = Δp = F × Δt (Impulse) Wave Calculations: v = f × λ (Wave speed = frequency × wavelength) T = 1/f (Period = 1/frequency)

6. Statistical Formulas

Mean (Average): μ = (Σx)/n Median: middle value of sorted data Mode: most frequent value Variance: σ² = Σ(x-μ)²/n Standard Deviation: σ = √(Σ(x-μ)²/n) Probability: P(event) = favorable outcomes / total outcomes Combination: C(n,k) = n!/(k!(n-k)!) Permutation: P(n,k) = n!/(n-k)! Factorial: n! = n × (n-1) × ... × 1 (0! = 1)

7. Financial Formulas

Simple Interest: I = P × r × t (Interest = Principal × rate × time) A = P(1 + rt) (Amount = Principal + Interest) Compound Interest: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) Where: A = final amount P = principal amount r = annual interest rate (decimal) n = number of times interest is compounded per year t = time in years Annuity: A = P × [(1 + r)^n - 1]/r Where A = future value, P = periodic payment Loan Payment: M = P × [r(1+r)^n]/[(1+r)^n - 1] Where M = monthly payment ROI (Return on Investment): ROI = (Final Value - Initial Value)/Initial Value × 100%
