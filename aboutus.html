About WideCalculator

Our Overview

WideCalculator is a free online platform dedicated to offering reliable and easy‑to‑use calculation tools for everyday users, students, professionals, and researchers. We cover finance, fitness & health, mathematics, science, unit conversion, and general daily calculators.

Our Mission

We aim to make complex calculations simple, fast, and accessible to everyone worldwide. Every tool on our website is designed to save your time and provide clear, accurate results without complicated steps.

What We Offer

  • Financial calculators: loans, mortgages, investment, interest & retirement planning
  • Health & fitness calculators: BMI, body fat, calories, diet and weight management
  • Math & science tools: fractions, percentage, statistics, geometry and converters
  • Daily utilities: age, time, date, password generator and character counters

Quality & Accuracy Promise

All calculators on WideCalculator are developed in‑house, manually checked, and fully tested before publication. We continuously optimize formulas and user experience to keep every result precise and trustworthy.

Contact & Feedback

If you find any errors, want to suggest new calculators, or need support, feel free to reach out through our official contact page. We welcome all constructive feedback to improve our platform.
